# Builder Readiness Assessment

## Purpose

This assessment evaluates whether an Apprentice is ready to progress to Builder.

---

## Prerequisite

Apprentice must have:
- Minimum 12 months in Apprentice role (exceptions require Master Builder approval)
- Builder mentor endorsement to proceed with assessment
- Portfolio of work demonstrating capability growth
