# Master Builder Readiness Assessment

## Purpose

This assessment evaluates whether a Builder is ready to progress to Master Builder.

---

## Prerequisite

Builder must have:
- Minimum 3 years as Builder (exceptions require organizational leadership approval)
- Successfully developed at least one Apprentice to Builder
- Track record of architectural decisions across products
- Endorsement from current Master Builder (if applicable)
